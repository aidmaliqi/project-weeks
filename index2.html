THE TWINING CAVES

Experience Via Ferrata abseiling

Cave Information

The Twining caves (in Albanian 'Shpellat Binjake') is the most attractive Via Ferrata and the most famous Twinning Cave in the Balkans. The Cave has two entry/exit corridors to the length of 70m, located in Bajshe, 14km from the town of Istog. It takes 30 minutes up to an hour to explore the Cave.
